SUSHI

Sushi rice is the heart and soul of the dish. Short-grain rice cooked with vinegar, sugar, and salt creates a slightly sweet and savory base with a unique sticky texture, perfect for shaping and holding ingredients.

FRIEDRICE

Fried rice is a classic dish of cooked rice stir-fried in a wok or frying pan, typically with additional ingredients like eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It's a popular staple in many cuisines, particularly in East and Southeast Asia, and holds different interpretations and variations depending on the region.
